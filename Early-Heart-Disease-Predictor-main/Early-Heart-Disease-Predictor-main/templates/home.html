Welcome to the Heart Disease Prediction System

This web application is designed to help predict the risk of heart disease based on various health parameters. By analyzing medical data, our machine learning model provides insights that can assist in early diagnosis and prevention.

The system uses advanced algorithms to evaluate key risk factors, including age, blood pressure, cholesterol levels, and lifestyle choices. While this tool does not replace medical advice, it serves as a helpful guide for users to monitor their health.
